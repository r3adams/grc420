Job Search Hints

    Before Starting Your Search

  • Update your resume and online portfolio.
  • Create a LinkedIn Profile (Note 1).
  • Design and print your business card.
  • Research the company.

    • Note 1. Be careful of the "add contacts" button because this will search through your contacts and email everyone to add you to their LinkedIn contacts.

    Business Cards

  • Include name, address, cell phone, email, and web site.
  • VistaPrint, design your business card, 100 cards for $18.00
  • Avery 28878 Laser Printer Business Cards, 90 cards for $8.27 on Amazon

    Interview Questions

  • Think through an impactful response to “Tell me about yourself,” emphasizing your experience and abilities.
  • Monster.com Top Interview Questions.
  • Monster.com Questions for the Interviewer
  • Monster.com Behavioral Interview Questions

    Package Design Skills

  • Make a 3D model of common package shapes.
  • Make a mockup by printing your design digitally on cover stock and manually cutting and folding.
  • Be familiar with flexo printing including press, plates, anilox rolls, and inks.
  • Recognize common packaging substrates including kraft paper, poly bags, labels, SBS, SUS, C1S, and corrugate.
