Pacdora Demo

Web Tool for Package Design

Pacdora is a paid site for creating package mockups. The site includes a variety of templates including boxes, cans, bottles, bags, and more. Users can place their own artwork on package mockups and use a multitude of preloaded symbols such as for recycling or package composition.

After mocking up a package, users can export their designs as static JPEG files, videos, or HTML iframes (such as the one below) to share with clients. By clicking and dragging the mouse over the mockup, viewers can rotate the package and view it from different angles, zoom in and out, and open and close the package.

Numerous YouTube videos explain how to use Pacdora.
